U-QASAR Methodology Webbook

This is the Prototype of the U-QASAR Methodology Webbook. The main objective of U-QASAR is to create a flexible Quality Assurance, Control and Measurement methodology to assess the quality of software development projects and their resulting products. The U-QASAR platform enables measurement of the quality of the process and product at any time.
