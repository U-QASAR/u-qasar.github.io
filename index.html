U-QASAR Methodology Webbook

This is the Prototype of the U-QASAR Methodology Webbook. The main objective of U-QASAR is to create a flexible Quality Assurance, Control and Measurement methodology to assess the quality of software development projects and their resulting products. The U-QASAR platform enables measurement of the quality of the process and product at any time.

This webbook is intended as a quick reference of the Methodology Workbook and is composed of 4 sections. The sections describe the most important steps (planning, controlling, and changingt) to implement quality models that reflect the U-Qasar methodology in your institution. Sections are divided in smaller parts. These parts contain clear guidelines and flyers that you can directly use.
